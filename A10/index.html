The first symbol map is one of the visualizations I submitted for our 9th Assignment. The map displays a symbol for each state illustrating the cost of housing via the size of the symbol. I would label the map as a Black Hat visualization because the symbols don't illustrate teh data clearly. In the smaller states the symbols overlap making them difficult to distinguish from one another. In the larger states the symbols are so far apart it's difficult to acuratley compare them. Symbols are also a poor choice to visualise this data set. To fix these problems I decided to make another map that uses a sequential color scale to represent the data. This alleviates the issue with over lapping symbols and makes it easier to compare data between states. It also makes it much easier to see trends in the data. I chose to show the monthly cost of Transportation from the data set, and by looking at the map you cna clearly see that the West and North East regions of the US are the most expensive

Changes Made:
  • Replaced Symbols with a sequential color scale
  • Improved title description
